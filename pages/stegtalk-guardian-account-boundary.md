## Guardian Boundary

The current StegTalk candidate includes local account profile and account session runtime. This does not yet constitute production identity, public account federation, device-bound guardian enforcement, or external recovery authority.

## Account Boundary

- Account profile readiness means local profile and linked-entity records exist.
- Account session readiness means local session event records exist.
- Account readiness does not imply external login authority.
- Account readiness does not imply cross-device account recovery.
- Account readiness does not imply production guardian enforcement.

## Required Interpretation

- `production_ready` remains false.
- Local account runtime is a prototype lane.
- Guardian/account notes are boundary documentation, not release approval.
