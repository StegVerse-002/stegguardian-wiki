## Pipeline Awareness

The connector-visible pipeline may describe bounded stages for media request declaration, evidence collection, transformation planning, receipt construction, replay verification, publication awareness, and downstream observability.

The existence of a stage map or publication record does not prove that a camera, microphone, broadcast service, provider, or public distribution path was used.

## Guardian-Relevant Controls

A media transition that could affect users, devices, accounts, or public distribution must preserve:

- explicit actor and device authority;
- purpose and destination scope;
- consent and revocation state;
- input, transformation, and output provenance;
- provider and tool boundaries;
- bounded validity windows;
- recoverability and failure receipts;
- and a reconstruction path.

Guardian eligibility or review does not itself authorize capture, transformation, publication, or broadcast.

## Non-Claims
